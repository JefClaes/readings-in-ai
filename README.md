# Readings in Artificial Intelligence

A curated guide to the foundational papers behind modern AI — neural networks, large language models, computer vision, and agentic systems.

Traces how the field arrived at its current state, from the perceptron through ReAct. Papers were selected for conceptual novelty. Together they represent roughly 100 pages of reading.
